Copyright Policy

Our commitment to protecting intellectual property rights and promoting fair use of music content.

1. Copyright Protection

We respect the intellectual property rights of others and expect our users to do the same. Our copyright policy covers:

  • Music tracks and compositions
  • Lyrics and written content
  • Album artwork and visuals
  • User-generated content

2. Fair Use Guidelines

Fair use allows limited use of copyrighted material without permission for purposes such as:

  • Commentary and criticism
  • News reporting
  • Teaching and research
  • Parody and transformation

However, each case is evaluated individually based on:

  • The purpose and character of use
  • The nature of the copyrighted work
  • The amount and substantiality used
  • The effect on the potential market

3. Copyright Infringement

The following actions are considered copyright infringement:

  • Unauthorized reproduction of copyrighted music
  • Distribution without proper licensing
  • Public performance without permission
  • Creation of derivative works without authorization

4. Licensing and Permissions

To use copyrighted music legally, you must:

  • Obtain appropriate licenses
  • Pay required royalties
  • Credit original creators as required
  • Comply with usage restrictions

5. Reporting Copyright Violations

If you believe your copyright has been violated:

  • Submit a DMCA takedown notice
  • Provide proof of copyright ownership
  • Identify the infringing material
  • Include contact information
