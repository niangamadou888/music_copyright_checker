DMCA Policy

The Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) is a US legislation that governs copyright issues in digital platforms. At Tube Music, we respect the intellectual property rights of others and are committed to complying with the DMCA 1998. This DMCA policy outlines a process for copyright owners to request the removal of infringing content from our gallery.

DMCA Takedown Request Procedure

If you believe that a video displayed in our gallery infringes your copyright, you can submit a DMCA takedown notice. Given below are the information you need to include in the takedown notice:

  • Identification of the copyrighted work: Please provide a clear description of the copyrighted material you claim is infringed.
  • Identification of the infringing material: Provide specific details about the song(s) you wish to have removed, including titles and links to our gallery.
  • Your contact information: Include your name, address, phone number, and email address.
  • A statement of good faith belief: Include a statement that you have a good faith belief that the disputed use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law. You must also include a statement that the information in the notification is accurate
  • Signature: The notification must be signed (either electronically or physically) by the person authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.

Counter Notification Process

If you believe that your content was wrongly removed due to a misidentification, you may file a counter-notice. It must include details like your contact information, the title of the removed material, a statement that it was removed due to error, and a signature. We will review counter-notifications carefully and may restore content if warranted.

Limitations of Liability

Our platform operates under the safe harbor provisions of the DMCA. We do not actively monitor user-uploaded content for infringement but will respond promptly to valid notices of claimed infringement. We reserve the right to terminate users who are repeat infringers under our DMCA policy.
