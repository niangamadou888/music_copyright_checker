How It Works?

Copyright checks can be frustrating and time-consuming for creators. Manually searching for potential copyright risks by cross-referencing large music databases can slow down the creative process. Even one wrong step could lead to severe consequences like strikes, demonetization, account suspension, etc.. That’s why we created our YouTube music copyright checker—to make the process simple and quick.

Paste the Link

First of all, paste the YouTube link to your music to our tool or enter its name. Then, our YouTube music copyright checker analyzes the link to directly check the audio from the source.

Database Scanning

Once you have submitted the search, our system extracts the audio and cross-checks it an extensive database that includes millions of songs and their licensing information. Don’t worry– the entire process just takes seconds to finish.

Get the Results

Soon, the screen will display a comprehensive report detailing the song's copyright status. It will also show the video thumbnail so that you can ensure that the tool analyzed the right audio.

Review and Adjust Your Content

With the detailed report in hand, you can easily identify areas of concern and make necessary adjustments before publishing your content.
